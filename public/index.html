High School / IB Art
High School / IB Art

The IB/IGCSE visual arts program is a thought-provoking course that helps develop students’ skills in problem-solving and divergent thinking. At The Art Studio Mumbai, we work towards making you technically proficient and confident as artists, through open-ended artmaking activities, that will enable you to express personal feelings, experiences and ideas. You will also learn about contemporary practices in the visual arts across the world, which will be needed for your extended essay as well as comparative studies…

Entrance Exam Training

Meet Our

Founder Director

Purnima Sampat, the founder, and director of the studio is a professional artist who has taught Art and Design in India as well as the US for more than 30 years. She has helped students prepare portfolios for specialist fields in art, design, and architecture with consistently excellent results.

In 2009, Purnima started Art4All at Marine Drive, Mumbai. Within a few years, due to an ever-increasing interest in the preparation of art, design, and architecture portfolios from students in Mumbai and across the globe, then she started The Art Studio Mumbai.
